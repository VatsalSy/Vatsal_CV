\cvitem{Supervisor:}{Prof. Arup Kumar Das}
\cvlistitem{Conducted full-scaled numerical simulations to explore the physics of liquid jet collision.}

\cvlistitem{Characterized atomization by collision of liquid jets: a result of Kelvin-Helmholtz instability.}

\cvitem{Supervisor:}{Prof. Arup Kumar Das}
